Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our digital business cards

How do digital business cards work? +

Digital business cards work by creating a shareable online profile that contains your contact information. You can share this profile via QR code, NFC tap, or a simple link. When someone scans your QR code or taps your NFC card, they're directed to your digital card where they can view your details, save your contact, or connect with you on social media.

Do recipients need an app to view my digital card? +

No, recipients do not need any special app to view your digital business card. When they scan your QR code or tap your NFC card, it opens directly in their web browser. They can save your contact information directly to their phone's address book with one click.

Can I update my information after sharing my card? +

Yes! One of the biggest advantages of digital business cards is that you can update your information at any time. When you make changes, all previously shared cards automatically update with the new information. You never have to worry about outdated contact details.

What's the difference between QR codes and NFC cards? +

QR codes are visual codes that can be scanned with a smartphone camera. NFC cards contain a small chip that can be tapped against an NFC-enabled smartphone to share your information. QR codes work with any smartphone camera, while NFC requires a phone with NFC capabilities (most modern smartphones have this). NFC is often faster and more convenient for in-person meetings.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans. During the trial, you can create your digital card, test all features, and share it with contacts. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Do you offer support in local languages? +

Absolutely! We provide support in Urdu, Pashto, and English. Our local team in KPK understands the specific needs of businesses in the region and can assist you in your preferred language.
