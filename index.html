Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JK Digital Cards in Pakistan.

How do I share my digital card in Pakistan?
You can share your JK Digital Card via WhatsApp (most popular in Pakistan), email, social media, or by having people scan your QR code. We provide multiple sharing options tailored for Pakistani users.
Can I update my information after receiving the card?
Yes! Business package subscribers can update their information anytime. Essential and Professional packages include one free update within 30 days. Perfect for when you change numbers or addresses.
How long does it take to receive my digital card?
Most cards are delivered within 24 hours. We understand the fast-paced business environment in Pakistan and prioritize quick turnaround times. Complex designs may take up to 48 hours.
Do you provide design support for Pakistani businesses?
Absolutely! We work with you to create a design that reflects your brand and appeals to the Pakistani market. All packages include design consultation and revisions to ensure your satisfaction.
Is there a setup fee or hidden costs?
No setup fees or hidden costs. The price you see is all you pay for Essential and Professional packages. The Business package is a yearly subscription with transparent pricing - no surprises!
Do you offer support in Urdu?
Yes! We provide support in both English and Urdu to ensure clear communication and better understanding for all our Pakistani clients.
